About Synthium Data

We're a data infrastructure company building the foundation for frontier AI.

Our Mission

To enable the next generation of AI models by providing the highest-quality training data — meticulously curated, decontaminated, and formatted for optimal model performance.

Our Team

A diverse mix of ML engineers, data scientists, domain specialists, and student engineers working together across time zones. We value rigor, curiosity, and ownership.

What We Do

⚙

Ingest & Filter

Raw data pipelines that deduplicate, decontaminate, and filter at petabyte scale.

✎

Annotate & Label

Instruction-response pairs, RLHF preferences, and safety labeling by expert annotators.

⚡

Format & Package

Sharded JSONL / HDF5 outputs ready for SFT, DPO, and RLHF training pipelines.
