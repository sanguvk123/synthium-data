About

We're the data infrastructure behind frontier AI.

Synthium Data provides the highest-quality training data for the next generation of AI models.

Our Mission

To enable the next generation of AI models by providing meticulously curated, decontaminated, and formatted training data for optimal model performance across SFT, DPO, and RLHF pipelines.

Our Team

A diverse mix of ML engineers, data scientists, domain specialists, and student engineers working together across 6+ time zones. We value rigor, curiosity, and ownership.

What We Do

End-to-end training data pipelines.

⚙

Ingest & Filter

Raw data pipelines that deduplicate, decontaminate, and filter at petabyte scale. We handle trillions of tokens from web crawls, code repositories, and domain-specific corpora.

✎

Annotate & Label

Instruction-response pairs, RLHF preferences, and safety labeling by expert annotators. We maintain rigorous quality rubrics with continuous inter-annotator agreement tracking.

⚡

Format & Package

Sharded JSONL, Parquet, and HDF5 outputs ready for SFT, DPO, and RLHF training pipelines. Custom schemas including ChatML, ShareGPT, and Alpaca formats.
